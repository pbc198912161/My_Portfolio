My Startup

Something I'm building from scratch — with purpose.

📱

Zuvik

Wake Up. Grow. Every Scroll Counts.
Visit Landing Page
"

I'm building an app which is not purely an educational app and not purely a social media app — it contains both components. It feels like social media but works like a teacher.

The Problem

The average person in India spends 4.2 hours every day scrolling through reels, shorts and feeds — and walks away with nothing real. People feel guilty, try to stop, and keep coming back. It's not a willpower problem. It's a design problem — and nobody has solved it the right way yet.

The Solution

Zuvik keeps the same familiar scroll but replaces the noise with short, AI-curated knowledge cards — each one takes just 15 seconds to read. The feed is personalised for every age group, from students to senior citizens, so every swipe leaves you smarter, calmer, or more inspired.

4.2h Avg. daily screen time in India
600M+ Smartphone users in India
All Ages 16 to 70+ — built for everyone
~0 min Useful knowledge from most reels

 We kept the scroll. We changed what fills it.
